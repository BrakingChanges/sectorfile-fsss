===== FSIA Departure =====

  * FSIA doesn't have a dedicated Departure Position, with Seychelles Approach (FSIA APP) assuming the role of Departure.


==== Departure Procedures ====

  * Aircraft are expected to contact Seychelles Approach after passing 1000 ft unless otherwise instructed by Seychelles Tower.
  * ATC may cancel a SID and provide direct routing.


==== Noise Abatement Procedures ====

  * Not available at this point.
